First foray into the origin of she-who-must-not-be named behemoth franchise. I haven’t ever read this series before, but have seen the movies. Despite knowing the author is a TERF and ignorant af, I wanna give the books an honest shot.

I loved sorcerers stone, having watched that movie time and time again - so I was excited to dive into this.

It’s a very simple book. It’s written in a very juvenile way, but that’s the point for sure. I found myself hooked to the story and enjoying myself despite the shallow prose. I wish I allowed myself to read this as a kid tbh. I liked it for what it was! Onto the next.
