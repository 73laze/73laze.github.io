the handmaids tale - margaret atwood

dates: [11.05 ~ 11.19.23]

rating: [*****]

I am not sure what else I can say about this book that hasn't already been said, but I really, really enjoyed this book. A truly necessary book. America has changed in the past decade and the world this book portrays could be on the horizon.

My favorite thing about this book was the writing itself. It was rough, and simple to start - but this was intended by the writer since the perspective we get is limited in a way. Her thoughts are quick and come at bursts. At times she also daydreams and escapes into echoes of the past to escape reality - all of which were beautifully written and break up the rigidness. I felt claustrophobic and downtrodden in Offreds mind. Dissociating and reciting the litanies with her as I went through the book. By the end, I felt like these bitch ass dusty disgusting men turning these women into vessels just might be their undoing. I hope to find out I'm right by the time I read the next book!
