dates: [03.21 ~ 4.22.24]

“In your whole life nobody has ever abused you more than you have abused yourself. And the limit of your self-abuse is exactly the limit that you will tolerate from someone else”.

Many, many times I closed this book and just sat with what I just read. Lots of things in here that are actually life / perspective changing. Highly recommend. Will be living the four agreements for the rest of my days.

let it be known: i have a strong disdain for organized religion and rigid belief systems - this is not that. there is some talk of a god(s) in here but you can still extract the messages in here and it have the same level of effect.

I got this for like, 4 bucks at a FIVE BELOW. MAYBE less. What a fantastic purchase.
